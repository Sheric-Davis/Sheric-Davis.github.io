Senior Project Storyboarding

This was originally going to be the anamatic for my senior project before teaming up with a fellow senior.



Story:


Meka is seen chasing fireflies. The field is lit by soft dusk light. In the background you hear a woman yell her name. Meka turns to look but a firefly catches her attention. She runs after it, suddenly entering the woods. She ducks under a branch and is suddenly in a different world. Across a short clearing a man is bent over attending a field of glowing flowers. Everything around them glows, including parts of the man. The trees have soft glowing patterns etched into them, they seem to pulsate to an unheard heartbeat. The young child gasps at the beautiful scene before her. The man (name Neon) hears a noise and turns towards the child. He is tall, with a semi-featureless mask like face- he is not a man but a monster. His hands are clawed, and his eyes glow softly. She lets out a strangled noise and stumbles back. He blinks slowly and the glow of the plants glow softens, the pulsing trees seem to pulse slower. He bends down to the garden he was attending and scoops a glowing flower out of the soft soil, putting it in a circular rock pot. He slowly walks towards the girl. She looks scared as he holds out his giant clawed hand to gift her the stone pot. Slowly she takes it. It is the same soft yellow color of the fireflies with a gentle mix of pink. Slowly, the monster man stands up and the child looks up at him. He is so tall, just like the trees. He holds out one clawed finger and she cautiously wraps her hand around it. They walk through the forest, the soft glow of the forest changing colors as the monster man wishes. The child is mystified. In the distance a woman yells the child’s name in a fearful voice, it is watery like they are underwater. The child looks at the monster man with wide eyes. His eyes glow the soft color of a firefly and he gently pushes the child though a hole formed by branches. The child is suddenly in her own world. She turns to look at the monster and his motions to her to go. She runs through the forest until she breaks through the forest line, hugging the beautiful glowing plant to her chest, running towards her name.
